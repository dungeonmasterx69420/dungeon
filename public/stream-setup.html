Install

Open the Stremio app from your Home Screen (the one you just added).

Tap the puzzle-piece icon (Add-ons) at the bottom.

At the top there's a search bar — tap it and paste your link.

The Dungeon appears — tap Install and you're done.

Tip: after installing, tap the home icon. Your Netflix, Disney+, HBO Max and Hulu rows will already be there.

3
That's it — start watching

The Dungeon is already installed on your account. Open Stremio from your Home Screen, and your Netflix, Disney+, HBO Max and Hulu rows are right there.

▶
Install an external player

On iPhone, Stremio needs a separate video player app to actually play streams. Install one of these free apps from the App Store first:

🟠 VLCFree · plays everything · recommended 🎬 OutplayerFree · smooth playback ✨ InfuseFree tier · polished interface

Then in Stremio, tap your profile picture (top-right corner) → Settings → scroll to External Player → pick the app you installed. Now when you tap a stream, it opens in that player and starts.

You're all set 🎬

Open Stremio from your Home Screen any time. Pick something, tap a stream, and it plays right in the app.
