Welcome to Dungeon — a private, invite-only streaming service. You're here because someone trusted you with an invite, and that means something. We keep this small and tight-knit on purpose.

Inside you'll find movies, TV shows, and live TV — all running on our own Jellyfin servers. Jellyfin is open-source media software, kind of like a self-hosted Netflix. It's fast, clean, and yours to use.

Step 1 — Get the App
Download Jellyfin
Jellyfin is the recommended app. Download it for your phone — you can cast from your phone to any TV.

On a TV? Download Jellyfin directly from your TV's app store — it's available on Fire TV, Android TV, Apple TV and Roku. Or cast from your phone using the cast icon inside the Jellyfin app. No setup needed.

Quick Connect — already signed in on your phone? On your TV open Jellyfin → Quick Connect, then enter the code shown into the Jellyfin app on your phone under Settings → Quick Connect. No need to type your password on the TV.

Other clients — Swiftfin (iOS/tvOS) and Streamyfin (iOS/Android) are alternatives if Jellyfin doesn't work for you.
Step 2 — Get Access
Redeem your credit
The app is downloaded — now you need your streaming credentials. Here's how to get them.
1
Go to your Dashboard and find the Credits section.
2
Tap Redeem and select DungeonStream for movies & shows, or DungeonCast for live TV.
3
The Dungeon Master will set up your account and email you your credentials. This is usually done within a few hours.
4
Once you have your credentials, open the app you downloaded, add https://dungeoncast.cc as the server, and sign in.
No credits yet? Contact support from your dashboard and the Dungeon Master will sort you out.
