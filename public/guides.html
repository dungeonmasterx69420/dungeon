Setup Guide

Get watching in 3 steps. Takes about 2 minutes.
Step 1 — Your credentials
1
Go to your DungeonStream page and copy your Server URL, Username and Password. You'll need these to sign in.
2
Your server URL is always:
https://dungeoncast.cc
Your Dungeon login (email + password) is only for enterdungeon.cc. Your streaming credentials are separate.
Step 2 — Download Jellyfin
1
Download the Jellyfin app for your device:
📱
Jellyfin
iPhone & iPad
📱
Jellyfin
Android
Step 3 — Connect
1
Open Jellyfin and tap Add Server. Enter https://dungeoncast.cc and tap Connect.
2
Sign in with your streaming username and password from step 1.
3
Tap Movies, Shows or Live TV and start watching.
Quick Connect — easy login on TV
Already signed in on your phone? Open Jellyfin on your TV, select Quick Connect, then enter the code shown on your TV into the Jellyfin app on your phone under Settings → Quick Connect. No need to type your password on the TV.
