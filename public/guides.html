Getting Started
Your server URL, username and password
▼
Your Credentials
  • 1
    Go to your DungeonStream page to find your credentials — Server URL, Username, and Password.
  • 2
    The Server URL is always https://dungeoncast.cc. You'll need this along with your username and password to log into Moonfin.
  • 3
    Download Moonfin on your device — it works on Android, iPhone, iPad, Fire TV, Android TV and Apple TV.
    Your DungeonStream credentials are separate from your Dungeon login. Use your Dungeon login (email + password) only on enterdungeon.cc.

All Devices
Moonfin Setup
Android · iPhone · iPad · Android TV · Apple TV
▼
Android Phone & Tablet
  • 1
    Download Moonfin from the Google Play Store.
  • 2
    Open the app and tap Add Server. Enter https://dungeoncast.cc as the server URL.
  • 3
    Sign in with your DungeonStream username and password.
iPhone & iPad
  • 1
    Download Moonfin from the App Store.
  • 2
    Tap Add Server and enter https://dungeoncast.cc.
  • 3
    Sign in with your DungeonStream username and password.
Fire TV & Fire Stick
  • 1
    Search for Moonfin in the Amazon Appstore and install it.
  • 2
    Open Moonfin and add server https://dungeoncast.cc.
  • 3
    Sign in with your DungeonStream credentials.
Android TV & Google TV
  • 1
    Download Moonfin from the Google Play Store on your TV.
  • 2
    Add server https://dungeoncast.cc and sign in.
Apple TV
  • 1
    Download Moonfin from the Apple TV App Store.
  • 2
    Add server https://dungeoncast.cc and sign in with your DungeonStream credentials.
Windows & Mac (Browser)
  • 1
    Open any browser and go to dungeoncast.cc.
  • 2
    Sign in with your DungeonStream username and password.
📡 DungeonCast
📡
All Devices
Getting Started
Live TV powered by Jellyfin — same app as DungeonStream
▼
Setup
  • 1
    Go to your DungeonCast page to find your credentials.
  • 2
    Download Moonfin and add server https://dungeoncast.cc — same app as DungeonStream, but log in with your DungeonCast credentials.
  • 3
    Find Live TV in the main menu. Tap any channel to start watching.
Watching Live TV
  • 1
    From the home screen tap Live TV to see what's on now.
  • 2
    Use the Guide tab to browse the full TV schedule and upcoming programs.
  • 3
    Tap any channel or program to start watching.
Tips
Live TV works best on a strong WiFi connection. If a channel buffers, go back and try again — some streams may be temporarily offline.
