Setup Guide

Your Dungeon access has two parts - DungeonStream for movies & shows, and DungeonCast for live TV. Here's how to set up each. Takes a couple of minutes.
🌊 DungeonStream - Movies & Shows
1
DungeonStream runs on the Stremio app. Download it for your device:

2
Open your DungeonStream page to find your Stremio account - the email and password we created for you.
3
In Stremio, tap Log in and enter that email and password. Your Dungeon content is already loaded on the account - no add-ons to install. Your movies and shows appear right away.
iPhone tip
Stremio on iPhone runs from Safari as a web app - our iPhone setup guide walks you through adding it to your Home Screen. iPhone also needs a free external player to play streams: install VLC, Outplayer, or Infuse from the App Store, then in Stremio tap your profile picture (top-right) → Settings → External Player and choose it.

2
Open Jellyfin and tap Add Server. Enter this and tap Connect:
https://dungeoncast.cc
3
Sign in with your DungeonCast username and password - you'll find these on your dashboard.
4
Tap Live TV and start watching.
Quick Connect - easy login on TV
Already signed in on your phone? Open Jellyfin on your TV, select Quick Connect, then enter the code shown on your TV into the Jellyfin app on your phone under Settings → Quick Connect. No need to type your password on the TV.
Your Dungeon login (email + password) is only for enterdungeon.cc. Your DungeonStream (Stremio) and DungeonCast (Jellyfin) logins are separate accounts - you'll find both from your dashboard.
