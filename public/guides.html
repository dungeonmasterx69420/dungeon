iPhone & iPad
iOS Setup
Add to home screen + Outplayer for local playback
▼
Standard Method
  • 1
    Open Safari on your iPhone or iPad and go to web.stremio.com.
    Must use Safari — other browsers don't support Add to Home Screen properly.
  • 2
    Tap the Share icon at the bottom of the screen (the box with an arrow pointing up), then tap Add to Home Screen. Name it "Stremio" and tap Add.
  • 3
    Open the app from your home screen and log in with your Stremio credentials. Make sure you log in so your addons and settings sync correctly.
  • 4
    Go to the App Store and download Outplayer — it's free. This lets Stremio open stream links in a proper video player.
  • 5
    Back in Stremio Web, tap the settings gear icon → scroll down to Advanced → find Stream links or External Player → select Outplayer.
    Once set, tapping any stream will hand it off to Outplayer automatically.
Tech-Savvy Option — Sideload the Full App
If you want the actual Stremio app (not the web version), you can sideload the official IPA. Stremio publishes it directly at stremio.com/downloads. Use AltStore or Sideloadly to install it — no jailbreak required. Note that free Apple IDs require re-signing every 7 days. If you're on iOS 14–17, TrollStore installs it permanently.

Fire TV · Android TV · Google TV
TV Setup
App store or sideload via Downloader
▼
Try the App Store First
  • 1
    On your TV remote, open the App Store / Play Store / Amazon Appstore and search for "Stremio". If it appears, install it and skip the rest of these steps.
    Stremio is now available on some Amazon Fire TV devices and Android TV via the Play Store. Try this first.
If It's Not in the Store — Use Downloader
  • 1
    Go to your TV's app store and install the Downloader app by AFTVnews. It's free and available on Fire TV and Android TV.
  • 2
    Before using Downloader, you need to allow unknown apps. On Fire TV: Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options → Install Unknown Apps → enable Downloader. On Android TV: Settings → Apps → Downloader → enable Unknown Sources.
  • 3
    Open Downloader, select the URL input field, and type: stremio.com then press Go. This loads Stremio's official website where you can download the APK directly.
    This goes straight to Stremio's official site — always the safest source for the APK.
  • 4
    On the Stremio downloads page, tap Download Now for Android TV / Fire TV. Wait for the download to finish, then tap Install.
  • 5
    Open Stremio and log in with your credentials. You can scan the QR code on screen using your phone to log in quickly. Your addons will sync automatically.
