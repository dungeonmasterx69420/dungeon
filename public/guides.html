Getting Started
Your server URL, username and password — and how to use them
▼
Your Credentials
  • 1
    Go to your DungeonStream page to find your credentials — Server URL, Username, and Password.
  • 2
    You'll need all three to log into Jellyfin on any device. The Server URL is always https://dungeoncast.cc.
  • 3
    Download the right app for your device from the list below, then enter your credentials when prompted.
    Your DungeonStream credentials are different from your Dungeon login. Use your Dungeon login (email + password) only on enterdungeon.cc.
How to Watch
  • 1
    Once signed in, browse Movies or Shows from the home screen. Search using the magnifying glass icon.
  • 2
    Tap a title to open it, then press Play. If multiple versions are available, the app will pick the best one automatically.
  • 3
    For TV shows, select the season and episode from the episode list below the title.

iPhone & iPad
iOS Setup
Jellyfin app or Infuse for the best experience
▼
Option 1 — Jellyfin App (Free)
  • 1
    Download Jellyfin from the App Store.
  • 2
    Open the app and tap Add Server. Enter https://dungeoncast.cc as the server URL.
  • 3
    Sign in with your DungeonStream username and password.
Option 2 — Infuse (Recommended)
  • 1
    Download Infuse from the App Store. The free version works great.
  • 2
    Go to Settings → Add Files → Jellyfin. Enter https://dungeoncast.cc and your credentials.
  • 3
    Infuse has a better UI and handles more formats natively without transcoding — great for 4K content.

Windows & Mac
Desktop Setup
Jellyfin Media Player or use your browser
▼
Option 1 — Jellyfin Media Player (Recommended)
  • 1
    Download Jellyfin Media Player from GitHub. Available for Windows and Mac.
  • 2
    Install and open it. Add server https://dungeoncast.cc and sign in.
  • 3
    Jellyfin Media Player uses MPV under the hood — it handles 4K and HDR content natively without transcoding.
Option 2 — Web Browser (No Install)
  • 1
    Open any browser and go to dungeoncast.cc.
  • 2
    Sign in with your DungeonStream username and password. Everything works right in the browser.
Which should I use?
Jellyfin Media Player handles high-quality streams more reliably and supports 4K without transcoding. The web browser is great if you're on a shared computer or don't want to install anything.

All Devices
DungeonCast Web Player
Watch live TV in your browser — no app needed
▼
Getting Started
  • 1
    Go to dungeoncast.cc in any browser.
  • 2
    Sign in with your DungeonCast credentials — these are separate from your DungeonStream login. The Dungeon Master sends them to you when you redeem an DungeonCast credit.
  • 3
    Browse channels using the Live TV tab, or use the Guide tab to see what's on now and what's coming up.
Adding to Your Home Screen (Mobile)
  • iOS
    Open dungeoncast.cc in Safari → tap the Share button → tap Add to Home Screen. It will open full-screen like a native app.
  • And
    Open dungeoncast.cc in Chrome → tap the three-dot menu (⋮) → tap Add to Home screen. Opens full-screen like an app.
Tips for best experience
Use Safari on iOS and Chrome on Android for the best compatibility. If a channel buffers, tap Back and try a different channel — some streams go offline temporarily. The web player works best on a strong WiFi connection.

Phone · Tablet · TV
IPTV Apps (Alternative)
Use a native IPTV app for the smoothest playback
▼
What You Need
  • !
    To use a native IPTV app you need your Xtream Codes credentials — a server URL, username, and password. These are different from your DungeonCast web login. Contact the Dungeon Master to get them.
Recommended Apps
  • 1
    Televizo (Android & Android TV) — Free, fast, excellent UI. Search "Televizo" in the Play Store. Add your source using Xtream Codes and enter your server URL, username and password.
  • 2
    TiviMate (Android TV / Fire Stick) — The best TV experience. Free version works great, premium adds multi-screen. Find it on the Play Store or Fire TV store.
  • 3
    IPTV Smarters Pro (iOS & Android) — Available on the App Store and Play Store. Add your source as Xtream Codes API and enter your credentials.
  • 4
    GSE Smart IPTV (iOS) — Good option for iPhone and iPad. Available free on the App Store.
How to Add Your Source (All Apps)
  • 1
    Open the app and look for Add Playlist or Add Source.
  • 2
    Choose Xtream Codes or Xtream Codes API as the type.
  • 3
    Enter your Server URL, Username, and Password exactly as provided by the Dungeon Master.
  • 4
    The app will load your channels automatically. This may take a minute on first load.
Which app should I use?
On Android phone or tablet, Televizo is the best free option. On a Fire Stick or Android TV box, TiviMate is the gold standard. On iPhone, IPTV Smarters Pro or GSE Smart IPTV both work well. All of these play streams natively so you get the smoothest possible experience.
